Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2025

1. Introduction

At Aivorize, your privacy is our top priority. We believe that you should always know what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it. This policy explains our practices in clear, simple language to ensure full transparency.

By using Aivorize and its tools, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. We encourage you to read it carefully and contact us with any questions.

2. Information We Collect

We do not require you to share personal data to use Aivorize tools. However, certain non-personal information may be automatically collected, such as:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device type and operating system
  • Anonymous usage statistics (via analytics)
  • Performance, crash, and error reports
  • Approximate location (country or region only, not precise)

This data cannot identify you personally. It helps us understand how users interact with our site so we can improve functionality, speed, and design.

3. How We Use Collected Data

We use collected information solely for the purpose of improving your experience. Specifically, we may use data to:

  • Optimize website performance and tool accuracy
  • Detect and fix errors or bugs
  • Analyze trends and improve user interface design
  • Maintain security and prevent misuse

We never sell, rent, or share your personal or behavioral data with advertisers or third parties for profit.

4. Cookies and Local Storage

Aivorize uses local storage and minimal cookies to enhance your experience. For example, we may save your theme (light/dark), favorites, or preferences directly on your device.

These small data files do not track you across the web and are stored locally for faster loading and customization. You can clear them anytime through your browser settings.

5. Third-Party Services

Aivorize may use trusted third-party services such as Google Analytics and Google AdSense to analyze traffic and display relevant ads.

These services may use cookies or similar technologies to collect anonymous usage information. All data shared with them is anonymized and handled according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy

6. Children’s Privacy

Aivorize is designed to be safe for students and teens. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided information, please contact us. We will take immediate action to delete such data.

7. Data Security

We use secure protocols (HTTPS) and follow modern security best practices to protect against unauthorized access, modification, or disclosure of information stored or transmitted on Aivorize.

While no system can be 100% secure, we continuously monitor and update our technology to maintain a safe browsing environment.

8. Your Rights & Choices

You have full control over your data and settings. You can:

  • Clear local storage and cookies anytime
  • Opt out of Google Analytics tracking (via browser add-on)
  • Request clarification about this policy

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect new features or changes in regulations. When we make updates, the “Last Updated” date at the top of this page will be revised.

You are encouraged to review this page regularly to stay informed about how we protect your data.
