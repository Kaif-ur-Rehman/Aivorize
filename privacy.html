Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it — because trust is the foundation of Aivorize.

Last Updated: October 2025

1. Overview

At Aivorize, your privacy is our top priority. We believe that every user deserves clarity, transparency, and control over their information. Aivorize does not collect personal details for basic tool usage — all tools can be used safely without signing up or logging in.

2. Information We Collect

While using Aivorize, we may automatically collect limited non-personal information to improve performance and user experience:

  • Browser and device type
  • Anonymous website usage analytics
  • Performance and error logs
  • Approximate region (not GPS-based)

This data is purely technical and does not identify individual users.

3. How We Use Data

We use the information collected solely to:

  • Fix bugs and improve site performance
  • Analyze anonymous usage patterns
  • Enhance the UI and user experience
  • Ensure compatibility across devices

We never sell or share your information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

4. Cookies and Local Storage

Aivorize uses your browser’s local storage to save your preferences — such as dark/light theme, favorites, and settings. This data stays on your device only and can be cleared anytime through your browser.

5. Third-Party Services

Aivorize may use services like Google Analytics or AdSense to understand site performance and serve safe ads. These third parties may use cookies to collect anonymous usage data.

You can review their policies here:

  • Google Privacy Policy

6. Children’s Privacy

Aivorize is designed to be safe for students and teens. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If a parent believes their child’s data has been shared accidentally, they can contact us for immediate deletion.

7. Data Security

We use HTTPS and secure technologies to keep your data safe. While no system is fully immune to risks, Aivorize takes every measure to ensure a secure, trusted browsing experience.

8. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally as we grow and introduce new tools. Any changes will be posted here with an updated "Last Modified" date.
